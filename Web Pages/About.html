Grayscale is a company that caters to the technology development, content writing, multi-lingual telesales and support, Web advertising, and multimedia marketing needs of local and international organizations in both public and private sectors.

Location & Facilities

The business location for Grayscale Business Consultancy & Marketing Services would be in Pineda, Pasig City, Philippines. We have multiple sites for our different respective services from multimedia marketing to telesales services for public and private organizations worldwide.

The Network

The Grayscale network is made up of very few subnetworks right now as we are only in the start-up phase. We operate and control most of our network components in our office but not all of them. This network consists of interconnected devices that provide network access, system distribution, and data transmissions to our data center all needed for business operation.

Cleanliness & Physical Health

In Grayscale, we always make sure the physical health of our employees as we deemed it as an important aspect of life. We have very strict rules here that are needed to be followed by every employee as well as employers.

Our offices are also being cleaned up daily with our professional cleaning department to make sure our people are going to work in an environment where they can feel clinically safe.

Business Computing

In this environment, we try to have a stable operation as much as possible. We provide secure computer-based communications throughout the network in our offices and along with the products we provide. We also track all monetary expenditures we do as we go along the business for maximum effectiveness in handling finances.

Security and Privacy

The company knows the importance of security and privacy as we all have different experiences in this regard. We are following a strict set of rules that must not be compromised in however way possible. There's also a training regime for our workers that makes it certain that they know the risks and the possible threats accompanying the wrong use of information. We know that these are core concepts of handling a business well and we make sure that this is one of the many elements we try to focus on as much as we could.
